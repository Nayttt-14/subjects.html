Mobile Legends

Mobile Legends is a 2-team game that aims to defeat the other team either by destroting their base or making them surrender. You pick a hero who has a designated role like a mage or marksman to be deployed in the batytlefield. In the battlefield, your task is to kill minions and monsters to level up and increase your power before the other team. You may encounter the enemy heroes of the other team. You can kill the enemy heroes to gain gold to further help to level up and defeat the other team.
